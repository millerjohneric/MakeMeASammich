### Ingredients
# Bloody Mary

![Bloody Mary.jpg](Bloody%20Mary.jpg)

**Ingredients:**
- 1 oz vodka  
- 3 oz tomato juice  
- 2 dashes red-hot sauce  
- 2 dashes green hot sauce  
- 1 dash Worcestershire sauce  
- 0.5 oz lemon juice  
- 1 pinch salt  
- 1 pinch pepper  
- 1 celery stalk

**Instructions:**
1. Combine vodka, tomato juice, hot sauces, Worcestershire sauce, and lemon juice in a shaker with ice.
2. Shake gently to mix.
3. Strain into a glass filled with ice.
4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
5. Garnish with a celery stalk.

1. Follow the mixing instructions provided in the source.
