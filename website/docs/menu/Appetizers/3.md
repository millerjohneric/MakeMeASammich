Deep Fried Deviled Eggs
• 12 peeled hard-boiled eggs
1 cup flour
3 eggs
1 cup panko bread crumbs
Oil for frying
4 Tbsp, mayonnaise
2 Tbsp, pickle relish
2 Tbsp, mustard
1 tsp hot sauce
Salt to taste
• Pepper to taste
Optional:
* Paprika, or seasoning of choice to top
Directions:
Heat cooking oil for deep-frying in a medium-size pan over medium heat.
Cut the hard-boiled eggs in half lengthwise. Gently separate the cooked egg white from
the yolk and remove to a medium bowl.
Coat the cooked egg white in flour, eggs, and panko bread crumbs. Carefully place eggs
into the hot oil. Fry for 2-3 minutes, flipping halfway through. Remove from pan and
drain excess oil on a paper towel.
Add mayonnaise, relish, mustard, hot sauce, salt, and pepper to the yolks and mash until
smooth. Fill a piping bag or plastic sandwich bag with yolk mixture. Pipe mixture back
into the fried egg white, dust with paprika, and serve!
