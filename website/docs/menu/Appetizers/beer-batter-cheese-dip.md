## RECIPE Beer Batter Cheese Dip

**Ready in:** [Insert Time]
**Serves:** [Insert Servings]

### Ingredients
SWEET CHICKEN
BACON WRAPS
Ingredients:
11/4 pounds boneless, skinless, chicken breasts
(about 4)
1 pound sliced bacon
I 2/3 cup firmly packed brown sugar
2 tablespoons chili powder
Instructions:
Cut chicken breasts into 1 inch cubes. Cut each bacon
slice into thirds. Wrap each cube with bacon and
secure with wooden pick. Stir together brown sugar
and chili powder. Dredge wrapped chicken cubes in
the mixture. Coat a rack and broiler pan with nonstick
cooking spray. Place chicken wraps on rack of broiler
pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes or until
bacon is crisp. Makes 12-15 pieces.
-Recipe provided by Pam Connelly
