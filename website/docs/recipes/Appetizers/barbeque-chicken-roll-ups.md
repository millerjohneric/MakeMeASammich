# BARBEQUE CHICKEN ROLL UPS

BARBEQUE CHICKEN ROLL UPS

	Pizza dough (refrigerated roll)
	Barbeque sauce (spread on pizza dough)
	Rotisserie chicken (shredded)
	Red onion
	Cheddar cheese (shredded)
	Fresh parsley
	Ranch dressing for dipping

Roll up and cut into slices. Bake at 425 degrees for 15 minutes.
