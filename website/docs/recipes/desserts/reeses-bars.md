# REESES BARS
Reeses Bars


3½     sticks of margarine
2        12 oz. packages of semi-sweet chocolate chips
1½     cups of peanut butter
2 ½    cups of powdered sugar
1 ½    cups of crushed graham crackers

Melt 1½ sticks of margarine with 1½ cups of peanut butter.
Stir in graham cracker crumbs and powdered sugar.  Mixture will be thick.
Spread into a 9 x 13 glass pan and press until smooth.
Melt chocolate chips and 2 sticks of margarine together.
Pour over peanut butter mixture and refrigerate.
