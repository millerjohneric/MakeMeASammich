# CHEESCAKE CHURRO

 CHEESECAKE CHURRO

•	2 pkgs. Cream cheese
•	1 Egg 
•	1 tsp. Vanilla
•	Mix together.
•	½ c. Sugar
•	2 tsp. Cinnamon
Mix together.
Spray bottom of pan and sprinkle with ½ of the cinnamon/sugar mixture.
•	1 can Crescent Rolls (roll out and place in bottom of pan)
Spread cream cheese mixture on top.
•	1 can Crescent Rolls (roll out and place on top of cream cheese mixture)
Sprinkle remaining half of cinnamon/sugar mixture on top.
Bake 30-35 minutes at 375 degrees.
