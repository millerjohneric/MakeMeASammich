# NO BAKE MIXED BERRY CHEESECAKE
No Bake Mixed Berry Cheesecake

CRUST:
Pre-made graham cracker crust (break it up)
Mix in 1-2 tablespoons of melted butter 
*Pat down the crust into a loaf pan that you’ve sprayed with oil then lay parchment paper down across a loaf pan.

Filling:
8 oz. cream cheese
1 can of sweetened condensed milk
Orange zest
Small bag of frozen mixed berries

Place into freezer for at least 4 hours.

To serve:
Warm a little jam on a plate and place the cheesecake on its side.

Top with:
Whip cream
