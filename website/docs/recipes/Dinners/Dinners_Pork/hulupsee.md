# HULUPSEE

HULUPSEE (European Cabbage Rolls)

Salt water with a little bit of vinegar. Core out cabbage and take off exterior leaves, boil for 15-20 minutes
Line pan with cabbage leaves to prevent burning

Rice (already cooked)
Ground pork
Salt 
Pepper
Lemon pepper
2 eggs

Place remaining cabbage leave on top
Bake for 1 ½ hours @ 425 degrees

Tomato Cream Sauce

Butter
Puree onions
Flour – to make a rue
Tomato sauce
Chicken stock 
Salt 
Cream

Pour sauce over cabbage rolls when done.
