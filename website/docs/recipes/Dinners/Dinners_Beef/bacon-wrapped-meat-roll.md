# BACON WRAPPED MEAT ROLL
BACON WRAPPED MEAT ROLL
	2 lbs. hamburger
	2 eggs
	1 c. bread crumbs
	2 tsp. salt
	1 tsp. pepper
	1 Tbsp. garlic powder
Mix and then flatten onto a pan lined with aluminum foil. Layer the following ingredients onto flattened hamburger mixture.
	Deli ham
	Provolone cheese
	Spinach
Roll in a loaf.
Line another pan with aluminum foil. Place raw bacon lengthwise on pan.
	Bacon
Roll the meat roll onto the bacon.
Bake rolled in foil at 350° for 25 minutes.
Unwrap and continue to bake at 450° for another 25 minutes.
